Benefits and Tips

Some benefits and tips of coffee

What are the benefits of drinking coffee?

  • Caffeine is the component of coffee that simulates and activates us.
  • Coffee improves your mood.
  • Coffee increases your physical perfomance.

Tips to enjoy coffee.

  1. Take in moderation.
  2. If you use a coffee maker and paper filters, moisten the coffee with cold water before putting on the hot water.
  3. It is recommended to grind it before preparing it, to prevent it from oxidizing.
